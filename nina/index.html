Nina

1950 Chris Craft 46' DCFB

Nina is a 46 foot Chris Craft DCFB powered by two Chrysler 440M gas motors. She has a modernized electrical system, ignition system, appliances, sonar and genset. Amenities include forward guest cabin and galley - midship is the main helm and salon - aft is the main cabin, bathroom and sun deck. The flying bridge contains a second helm and another sun deck.
