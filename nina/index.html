Nina

1950 Chris Craft
DCFB

Nina is a 46 foot Chris Craft DCFB based out of Seattle WA. She has a modernized power plant, electrical system, genset, and navigation system. Amenities include a flying bridge, galley, salon, stateroom, guest cabin, two helms, one bathroom and a couple sun decks.
