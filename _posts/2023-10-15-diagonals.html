Illustration, Research, Branding

An experiment to better understand the needs of creators, and have a little fun in the process

Participation is crucial for understanding

Interviews and market research are great and all, but just digging in and becoming every persona possible is also a great tool for ideation and understanding. Plus NFTs are fun and this is the perfect excuse to make art and learn about the attention economy.

The plan

Try to avoid making a plan, go with the flow and let the experience inform. Just be cool, pay attention and make it up as you go. Collect as much alpha as possible and apply it to xrp.cafe.

The art

Three thousand mostly generative artworks based on a variety of tropes and themes. Once you start to think about art production in a systematic way a lot of really interesting opportunities surface. So much of the creative process is accidental, the generative process amplifies these happy accidents. It really is a great tool for discovery.
